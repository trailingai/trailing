How It Works

Capture the evidence path from agent action to auditor-ready proof.

Ingest

Stream agent runs, approvals, tool calls, and policy events into one independent record of execution.

Verify

Detect gaps, chain actions to reviewers, and confirm integrity before incidents or model-risk reviews escalate.

Prove

Hand auditors a clear ledger of what happened, who approved it, and which controls were enforced.
